FlatFolder
Description

Flatten out them folders! This currently just makes the folders more flat looking. It may have additional preferences in the future but that's still up in the air at this moment in time. Tested on iOS 12 devices only. Compiled for arm64e but not yet tested on one. Use on pre-iOS 12 and arm64e devices at your own risk.
